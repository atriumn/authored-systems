Placeholder. The scaling objection: authoring the system sounds like writing a giant if/else tree. The answer is AI bootstrapping — observe behavior, propose rules, human approves. The system grows organically from what people are already doing, not from a requirements document.

Key points:
- Start with one process, not the whole operation
- AI proposes policies from observed patterns ("you always route HVAC to TechCorp for buildings over 50 units")
- Human approves, edits, or rejects — then it's a rule
- The 80/20 split: handle 80% deterministically, route 20% to humans with full context
- The fallback is itself a policy: "if no rule matches, escalate to a human"
