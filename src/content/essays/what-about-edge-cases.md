Placeholder. The "real operations are messy" objection.

Key points:
- The system doesn't need to handle every case autonomously
- 80% deterministic, 20% human — with full context, not blind escalation
- "If no rule matches, escalate" is itself a policy — authored, not inferred
- Edge cases are where agents are most dangerous — guessing in novel situations
- Edge cases are where authored systems are safest — they know what they don't know
- The Ledger captures edge cases, AI proposes new rules for recurring ones
- Over time, the 80/20 shifts toward 90/10, then 95/5 — organically
